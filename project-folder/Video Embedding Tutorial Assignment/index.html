<h1>How to Embed a YouTube Video</h1>
<p>
This tutorial shows how to add a YouTube video to a website.
</p>

<p>
Go to YouTube and open the video you want to use.
</p>
<img src="images/step1.png" alt="Open YouTube video">
<h2>Step 2: Click the Share button</h2>
<p>
Click the Share button below the video.
</p>
<img src="images/step2.png" alt="Click share button">
<h2>Step 3: Copy the Embed code</h2>
<p>
Choose Embed and copy the HTML code.
</p>
